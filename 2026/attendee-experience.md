#### 3DV 2026 Attendee Experience

This year, 3DV has partnered with DayOf, a startup founded by members of the computer vision community, to help you experience the best of Vancouver while you're here. Check out the 3DV experience site at [https://www.dayof.travel/3DV2026](https://www.dayof.travel/3DV2026). In addition to discovering and booking great tours and dining experiences, two exclusive offers are available just for 3DV attendees:

##### Culinary Networking Experience
A 2.5-hour food tour through one of North America's top culinary destinations, exclusively for 3DV attendees. Enjoy incredible food while meeting colleagues and collaborators from across the field. There are opportunities each on Saturday and Sunday after the poster sessions.

##### Walking Tour of Vancouver (coming soon!)
A 2 hour walking tour just for 3DV attendees, departing from the convention center Friday after the poster sessions. Explore the city and learn about the culture, history, and sites that make Vancouver one of the most diverse and popular destinations in Canada.

These experiences are not included with conference registration. You can browse and book them directly through the DayOf site. They are highlighted with amber frames around the listings. Spots are limited, so book early.

We're working on getting more exclusive offers for 3DV attendees as well--stay tuned.
