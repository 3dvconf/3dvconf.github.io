The 3DV conference takes place at the [Thessaloniki Concert Hall](https://www.tch.gr/default.aspx?lang=en-GB){:target="_blank"} @ M2 building.

Designed by renowned architect [Arata Isozaki](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arata_Isozaki){:target="_blank"}, the M2 Building is a world-class, technologically advanced conference center. 
Situated on the Thessaloniki coast, it offers delegates seamless connectivity to the city center and international airport. 
On-site amenities include an underground parking lot with 230 spaces and the high-end Allegro restaurant at the rooftop.

<br>

### Main Hall

The "Aimilios Riadis" Hall is designed for high-end presentations with a capacity of 500 seats. Equipped with state-of-the-art audio-visual technology and superb acoustics to ensure a premium speaker experience.

### Foyer

A sophisticated, multi-level foyer (~500+ m²) flooded with natural light and breathtaking views of the Thermaic Gulf and Mount Olympus.
The expansive foyer area easily accommodates many poster boards and sponsor booths in a single, high-traffic area. 
The glazed facade and open architecture create an inviting environment for networking during the peak conference hours.
