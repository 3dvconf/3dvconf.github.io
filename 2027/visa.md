#### Visa Conditions

[Greece](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Greece){:target="_blank"}  is a member of the [European Union](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/European_Union){:target="_blank"} and the [Schengen Area](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Visa_policy_of_the_Schengen_Area){:target="_blank"}. 

To check if you require a valid visa to enter Greece and for more information on visa applications please refer to [this list of Greece's Ministry of Foreign Affairs](https://www.mfa.gr/en/services/visas-for-foreigners-traveling-to-greece){:target="_blank"}.  
More information on Visas and travel requirements can be found [here][here](https://www.mfa.gr/en/services/visas-for-foreigners-traveling-to-greece/countries-requiring-or-not-requiring-a-visa){:target="_blank"}. 

If required, please apply for a visa as early as possible.

#### Visa Invitation Letters
